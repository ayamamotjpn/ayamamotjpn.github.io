Wyckoff positions of Space groups for Dihedral quasicrystals III : Octagonal groups

Wyckoff positions of octagonal space groups are given.

Abstract: Wyckoff positions of five-dimensional octagonal space groups are calculated to facilitate the model building and structure analysis of quasicrystals. There are 7 centrosymmetric space groups, and yy non-centrosymmetric ones.

1 Introduction

The octagonal quasicrystals (o-QCs) are rearly found but the formation of an octagonal quasicrystal in soft matter is expected under some simple potential. An octagonal two dimentional (2D) photonic quasicrystal (PQC) is simply formed by the use of only one occupation domain.[5, 6, 3, 4, 1] The symmetry of 2DPQCs is given by a 4D space group which is not tabulated in this paper. The 5D octagonal spacegroups tabulated in this paper are applicable to 3DPQCs and QCs.

2 Calculation Method

The program Carat implemented in the program package GAP is applied to the octagonal space groups as same as previous two papers. All dihedral 5D space groups are classified by its rotation axis with maximum order. It is denoted as n. When n is a power of 2, such as tetragonal or octagonal point groups, the number of non-equivalent space groups is large compared with other cases.[2] There are 16 space groups with point group 8/mmm(831mm) where as there are 4 space groups with 10/mmm(1071nn) and 3 space groups with 12/mmm(1251mm) and 8 space groups with point group 8mm(83mm) while 4 with 10mm(107mm) and 3 with 12mm(125mm) etc.
